Pudim Flan

Description

The Pudim Flan is a wonderful marriage between the flavors of Vanilla, Caramel, Milk and Eggs.

It is believed that this recipe comes from Roman times, when it was called cha mado tyropatina.

Ingredients

12 Eggs
550g Sugar
1Lt Milk
1 Cinnamon Stick
1 Lemon Peel Slice
100Ml Water

Preparation

In a pan, put 300g of sugar and 100ml of water. Bring to the stove and heat it up.
Keep stirring and let boil until the mixture turns into caramel.
When it is done, coat the inside of the mould where the pudim will be made with the caramel, making sure to coat the insides evenly.

In another pot heat up the milk, cinnamon stick and lemon peel
Keep stirring for a couple of minutes but do not let it get to boil.

Crack the eggs into a bowl and add the remaining 250g of sugar.
Stir a bit until its fairly well mixed.
Strain the milk on a thin meshed strainer.
In a drizzle and always stirring, add the milk to the eggs.

Pour the mixture into the mould and seal it.
Put the mould inside a water filled pot so it can cook Bain Marie

Bring the pot inside a pre heated oven at 200CÂº and cook for 1h.
After 1h take the pot out of the oven and wait until it fully cools down.
After its at room temperature, take it out of its mould and its ready to serve.
